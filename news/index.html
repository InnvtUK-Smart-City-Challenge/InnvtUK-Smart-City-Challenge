1st February 2018, London

Kick-Off Meeting
On 1st February 2018, all partners of the project consortium attended the Innovate UK kick-off meeting in London. Queen Mary University of London, Vision Semantics Ltd, Nanjing Science and Technology University and Jiangsu Sanleng Smart City & IOT System Co. Ltd gave an introduction of their organisations. As the UK project lead organisation, Vision Semantics also gave a presentation on the project overview and their plan on project management. The meeting concluded with a general discussion between all partners and the Innovate UK project officer.
