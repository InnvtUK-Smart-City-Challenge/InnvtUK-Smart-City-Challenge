Quarter 4 Project Meeting
The Quarter 4 project meeting was held on 29th October 2018 at the Mile End campus of Queen Mary University of London. All UK partners updated their project progress, with Quarter 3 deliverables from both the UK and China partners all delivered on time. The project progress is on track and further progress will be discussed in the next quarterly meeting at the beginning of February 2019.

Quarter 3 Project Meeting
The Quarter 3 project meeting was held on 29th October 2018 at the Mile End campus of Queen Mary University of London. All UK partners updated their project progress, with Quarter 3 deliverables from both the UK and China partners all delivered on time. The UK project team also shared a summary of the annual project meeting in Nanjing, China between 15th and 16th October. The project progress is on track and further progress will be discussed in the next quarterly meeting at the beginning of February 2019.

Quarter 2 Project Meeting
The Quarter 2 project meeting was held on 14th August 2018 on the Mile End campus of Queen Mary University of London. All UK partners presented their progress update. Quarter 2 deliverables from both the UK and China partners were delivered on time. The project progress was on track. Further progress will be discussed in the next quarterly meeting in the end of October 2018. The UK project teams will attend the annual project meeting in Nanjing this October and share the meeting details with the UK project officer in the quarterly meeting.

Quarter 1 Project Meeting
The Quarter 1 project meeting was held on 9th May 2018 on the Mile End campus of Queen Mary University of London. All UK partners (VSL and QMUL) gave the presentations on their progress update. VSL showed demo videos of the system testing, which look promising. Quarter 1 deliverables from both the UK and China partners were delivered on time. The project progress was on track. Further progress will be discussed in the next quarterly meeting in August 2018.

Kick-Off Meeting
On 1st February 2018, all partners of the project consortium attended the Innovate UK kick-off meeting in London. Queen Mary University of London, Vision Semantics Ltd, Nanjing Science and Technology University and Jiangsu Sanleng Smart City & IOT System Co. Ltd gave an introduction of their organisations. As the UK project lead organisation, Vision Semantics also gave a presentation on the project overview and their plan on project management. The meeting concluded with a general discussion between all partners and the Innovate UK project officer.
