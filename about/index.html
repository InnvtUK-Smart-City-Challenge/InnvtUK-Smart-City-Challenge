Funded by the Innovate UK and Jiangsu Science and Technology Department , this two-year project will bring about transformative video analytics capabilities to commercial intelligent video systems in large-scale smart urban environments using distributed cameras and multi-sensors. This enhances both a "digitally-enabled city" with smart infrastructure and connected digital information processing and a more "resilient city", benefiting the quality of urban services including transport and citizens’ security.
The UK partners (VSL/QMUL) are developing world-class intelligent video analytics with patented commercial software systems already deployed by actual end-users from law enforcement agencies in the UK, the US and Australia. Their Chinese partner in Jiangsu (Sanleng) is a market leader in providing networked video camera systems for public safety and smart city transport management in the Chinese market.

UK Team
Vision Semantics Ltd.

Vision Semantics Ltd. (VSL) was founded in 2007, a leading UK video analytics SME that holds key patents and extensive collaborative projects. The video semantic content analysis technologies based on activity and behaviour recognition developed by VSL is recognised by a wide range of governmental and commercial users in the video analytics market and the critical infrastructure security sector.

Queen Mary University of London

Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) is one of the UK's leading research-focused higher education institutions within the Russell Group. Queen Mary has made a strategic commitment to the highest quality of research. The results of the most recent national assessment of research – the Research Excellence Framework (REF 2014) – have confirmed their place in the top group of research-led universities in the UK. Overall ranked 9th in the UK among multi-faculty universities and 5th in the UK for the percentage of their 3* and 4* research outputs. The Computer Vision Group was founded in 1993, internationally renowned for video behaviour analysis & person re-identification, with 3 Professors, 4 Reader and Senior Lecturers, 35 Postdoc & PhDs.

China Team
Jiangsu Sanleng Smart City & IOT System Co. Ltd

Jiangsu Sanleng Smart City & IOT System Co. Ltd (Sanleng) was founded in 2001, a leading Chinese intelligent video systems manufacturer in Jiangsu with various solutions deployed by both government and commerce; Running Jiangsu Traffic Information Intelligent Video Analysis Engineering and Technology Research Center; Representative projects: CKE Highway CCTV Surveillnace (Sri Lanka) and 13KV Toril Substation Security (Philippines).

Nanjing University of Science and Technology

Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST) is one of the national key universities under the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology of China. The Key Laboratory of Image and Video Understanding for Social Safety (founded in 2012) and The Social Security Technology Jiangsu Synergy Innovation Center (founded in 2014), of 10 Professors, 5 Associate Professors, 8 Lectures and Researchers, and 30+ PhDs, well known for surveillance video/image analysis.
